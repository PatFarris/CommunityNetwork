Somerville Mentorship Program

Community Network

Supporting young Somerville adults.

The Community Network is a simple way for Somerville residents, alumni, and local professionals to support young adults who are taking their next steps in education, careers, and personal growth. You will receive one email per month with clear, low-lift ways to help, from introductions to advice to small acts of support.

What you will get each month

Profiles

Profiles of our mentees and community members

Opportunities

Simple opportunities to collaborate or offer support

Updates

Updates on our collective community impact

Invitations

Occasional invitations for small volunteer tasks
